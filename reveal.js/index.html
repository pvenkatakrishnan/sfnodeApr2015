Variety of logging requirements

  • Need to log into various systems
  • Ability to tag logs
  • Similar things logged across various systems
  • Ability to propagate data into logger without having to pass `req` around
  • Want to log with a simple one line interface
    • logger.error(data, 'UI');

    vs

    fooLogger.error(data); barLogger.error(data); //or logger.fooLogger.error(data); logger.barLogger.error(data);
  • Easy setup and configuration of logging across various environments
  • Want to easily pluck data from a payload to log
